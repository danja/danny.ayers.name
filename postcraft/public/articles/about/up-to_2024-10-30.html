I asked Claude :

What is the toki pona "pali wan, kama pini tu." in English?

The Toki Pona phrase "pali wan, kama pini tu." can be translated to English as: "Do one task, finish two." This phrase uses the following Toki Pona words: pali: work, do, make wan: one kama: to become, to arrive, to bring about pini: end, finish, complete tu: two The meaning conveys the idea of efficiency or accomplishing more than expected - by doing one task, you end up completing two things. It's similar to the English saying "killing two birds with one stone."
