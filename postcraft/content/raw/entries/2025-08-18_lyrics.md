# Lyrics

I have a tune prepared that really needs a voice on top. The problem is I lack lyrics.

Stream of consciousness while it was playing gave me the following :
```
I've not got the words for how I feel
or how I deal with the situation

The lights are one but there's no-one home
and I often wonder about you

forgive me my exasperation  
I guess I'll find the way
when I'm not looking

thank you for the salad.
```
